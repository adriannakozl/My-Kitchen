My Kitchen, your shopping list in 1 minute !

My Kitchen suggests creative but simple recipes and details all the ingredients necessary, adapted to your taste and preferences. More than just being a source of diversified recipes, My Kitchen fills your shopping list with the right and necessary ingredients. allows you to order directly your groceries on the website or the app.

Our mission

At My Kitchen, we want to return to the basics of everyday life. We put the pleasure of cooking and eating well at the center of our activity. Eating better, wasting less and consuming sustainabily are our key words. By allowing yourself to save time, you get back to basics.

Our vision

To allow everyone to cook better, simpler and healthier to save time and avoid food waste. In a world full of stress, between work, studies, family and friends, transforming the kitchen in a space of relaxation and socialisation is our vision. The environmental aspect is also very close to our heart, preventing waste in households and encouraging people to get creative with their leftovers!
